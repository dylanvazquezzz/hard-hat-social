# Requirements: Hard Hat Social — v1.3 UX & Trade Expansion

**Defined:** 2026-03-12
**Core Value:** A contractor can find and contact a verified sub in their trade within 5 minutes — zero unverified people, no spam, no guesswork.

## v1.3 Requirements

Six feature areas from user feedback after v1.2 launch.

---

### Auto-Deploy on Phase Completion

- **DEPLOY-01**: After any phase is approved by the user, GSD automatically commits, pushes to GitHub, and the Vercel production deployment updates hardhatsocial.net without manual steps.

---

### Bug Fix — 400 Error on Applications Query

- **BUG-06**: The 400 Bad Request on `GET /rest/v1/applications?select=status&user_id=eq.{id}&status=eq.pending` is diagnosed and fixed. Likely cause: RLS policy blocks the anon/authenticated role from filtering by `user_id` on the applications table, or the query filter order is wrong. Fix must not regress admin access to all applications.

---

### Directory Filter Expansion

- **FILTER-01**: The `/contractors` directory filter panel includes insurance and certification filters in addition to trade and location — users can filter by "Has General Liability Insurance", "Has Workers Comp", and by specific certification names (e.g. "AWS Certified Welder", "EPA 608").
- **FILTER-02**: Filters compose correctly — selecting trade + cert returns only contractors who match both.
- **FILTER-03**: Filter UI is clean on mobile — no horizontal overflow, filters collapse or stack correctly at 375px.

---

### Job Posting UX Overhaul

- **JOBS-05**: Navigating to `/profile` when the user is a GC defaults to the Posts tab with the Jobs sub-category pre-selected — no extra clicks required.
- **JOBS-06**: The job post composer has dedicated structured fields: Title, Trade, Pay Rate (text, e.g. "$45/hr" or "Negotiable"), Location (city + state), Duration/Timeline, Description. These replace or augment the freeform content field.
- **JOBS-07**: Posted jobs render the structured fields (pay, location, duration) clearly on the JobCard — not buried in a text blob.

---

### GC Recent Contacts in Hired Flow

- **JOBS-08**: When a GC opens the "Mark Hired" modal, a "Recent Contacts" section appears at the top showing contractors the GC has previously hired (up to 5, ordered by most recent hire date) — makes repeat hiring fast.
- **JOBS-09**: The full contractor search in SubSelectorModal remains available below the Recent Contacts section.

---

### Drywall Trade

- **TRADE-01**: "Drywall" is a selectable trade on the application form (`/apply`) alongside the existing trades.
- **TRADE-02**: "Drywall" appears as a filter option in the `/contractors` directory trade filter.
- **TRADE-03**: "Drywall" appears in the "Browse by Trade" section on the homepage.
- **TRADE-04**: Verification requirements for Drywall are documented: state contractor license + proof of general liability insurance.

---

### Homepage Hero Redesign

- **HOME-01**: The hero section has a full-bleed background of black-and-white trade photos (HVAC, welding, plumbing, drywall, electricians) behind the headline "The verified network for all tradespeople".
- **HOME-02**: "Browse by Trade" is a clearly separated section below the hero, divided by a horizontal rule or visual break consistent with how "How it works" is separated — not visually merged with the hero.
- **HOME-03**: Hero text remains fully readable over the photo background (dark overlay or text shadow).
- **HOME-04**: Hero layout is responsive — photo background scales correctly on mobile, text doesn't overflow.
